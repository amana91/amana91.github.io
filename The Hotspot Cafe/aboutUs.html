About Us


The Hotspot Café, is a place for students and young people to come work, socialise or even relax. This allows them to work remotely from a place of comfort and peace. The Hotspot Café allows,
for everyone to essentially come for fun and work, there will be a quiet zone for those who want to come for work, a zone for socialising or relaxing and another for recreation.


Each zone to fit the needs of young people who want a change from home. Creating a space like this will encourage young people to work in an environment where they are comfortable and a sort of escape from reality for some as well.
Having a workspace and café in one will enable all to have an experience like no other.

See what we have to offer below.




What we offer:

There are booths to use for meetings and group work as well as indivudual spaces.
